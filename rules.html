1v1 Rules & Tierlist Info

~ ~ ~ 1v1 Rules for Certain Kits ~ ~ ~

  • Both players select the kit for the 1v1.
  • Play best of 3 on all kits except for Sword & Axe which are best of 6.
  • mcpvp.club only.
  • For the 1v1 to count for the tierlist, Leo must be present.
  • Health must be visible and not hidden.

~ ~ ~ Tierlist Info ~ ~ ~

  • 1st place: 5 points
  • 2nd place: 3 points
  • 3rd place: 2 points
  • 4th place and under: 1 point
